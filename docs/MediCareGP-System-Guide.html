01

How it all fits together

One system, two sides — and they're always in sync.

Think of the system as having two sides that share the same information. When reception does something on one side, the doctor sees it instantly on the other.

The patient side

  • Browse the website & book online
  • Check themselves in with a QR code
  • Join a video call from a private link
  • No login — just a page or a link

The practice side

  • Reception runs the diary & front desk
  • The doctor writes notes & orders tests
  • Billing, documents, reports
  • Login required — staff only
One source of truth. Because both sides share the same records, an online booking appears for reception immediately, and a note the doctor writes is available everywhere it's needed. Keep records accurate and everyone benefits.

04

Patient records

The complete file for every patient — kept in one place.

What's in a file

  • Personal & contact details, account holder, next of kin
  • Medical aid scheme, plan & membership
  • Allergies, chronic conditions, current medication
  • Past surgeries, family history, lifestyle
  • Vitals each visit — and automatic BMI

Use it well

  • Allergies show as warning banners on appointments — keep them tidy.
  • Each patient gets a file number automatically.
  • If it says a patient already exists, search by ID number — don't make a duplicate.

09

Video consultations

Built-in video calls — nothing to install for the patient.

For online appointments, the doctor joins from the dashboard and the patient joins from a private link reception sends them (e.g. by WhatsApp or email). No app, no third-party service.

Video consultation
You
● Connected
🎤 🎥 End call
  • The doctor can choose the on-screen layout (floating, split or full screen) and write notes during the call.
  • Reception copies the patient's join link from the appointment and sends it.
If a call won't connect ("Connecting…" that never finishes), staff can open the built-in connection-test page — it tells you straight away whether the calling service is healthy. Check that before escalating to tech support.

First things to check before raising a ticket.

Good to know

🔐 Patient links are private

Video, check-in and results links are meant only for the person they're sent to. Don't post them publicly.

💳 No online payment

The practice always settles payment directly with the patient — nothing is charged on the website.

🔄 Always in sync

What reception does is instantly visible to the doctor, and vice versa. Accurate records help everyone.

🚪 Two ways in

Patients use the public website and links; staff log in to the practice dashboard.
